# Architect Support Agentic Platform (moved)

## Why

The design grew beyond SpecChat's scope. SpecChat is a spec language (SpecLang, profiles, validators, canvas rendering). ASAP is an agentic support platform for the BTABoK practitioner across all four BTABoK models. ASAP depends on SpecChat through the MCP server surface; SpecChat is unaware of ASAP.

The fork decision was recorded on 2026-04-18 at [Architect-Support-Agentic-Platform.md §0](../../asap/WIP/Architect-Support-Agentic-Platform.md). SpecChat Waves 1 through 4 remain in this repository. ASAP Waves (1 and 2, corresponding to the original Waves 5 and 6) ship from the new repository.
